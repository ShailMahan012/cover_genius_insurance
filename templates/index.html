About Us

Autocover is an online vehicle insurance platform where drivers in South Africa on E-hailing and delivery apps such as ( Uber, Bolt, UberEats, etc) can purchase the most suitable car insurance policy for E-hailing and delivery job/business.

We help E-hailing and delivery app drivers find the best affordable car insurance in the market that is suitable for E-hailing driving job/business.

Our partnerships with the best vehicle insurers in South Africa has enabled us to implement innovative price comparison technology in order to aggregate and provide the most affordable comprehensive car insurance for E- hailing and delivery drivers.

With our seamless process our customers can purchase E-hailing comprehensive car insurance online from partner insurers on our platform with just a few clicks and we would have the comprehensive car insurance certificates sent to their email address within 24 hours.

Our customers can be rest assured that our partner insurers will cover the cost of any repairs when they file a claim through us, in the event that the insured vehicle has been damaged due to an accident.
